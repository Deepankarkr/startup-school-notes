# **Lecture 6: Growth (Alex Schultz)** 

---

## **Core Idea**

Growth = **retention first**, everything else later.

---

## **1. What Drives Growth**

* Great product → users → retention
* **Retention = most important metric**

If retention curve:

* Flattens → viable business
* Goes to zero → no product-market fit

---

## **2. Retention Analysis**

* Track **cohorts** (users over time)
* Look at % returning users

Good:

* Initial drop → then stabilizes

Bad:

* Continuous decline → users leave

Rule:
→ If retention is bad, **don’t focus on growth tactics**

---

## **3. Product-Market Fit First**

* Most startups think they have it → they don’t
* Growth without PMF = waste

---

## **4. What is “Good Retention”**

* Depends on industry

Examples:

* Social: ~80%+
* E-commerce: ~20–30%

Use:

* Market size estimates
* Benchmarks

---

## **5. Growth Teams**

* Early startup → **no growth team**
* Entire company = growth team
* CEO = head of growth

---

## **6. North Star Metric**

* One key metric guiding everything

Examples:

* Facebook → Monthly active users
* WhatsApp → Messages sent
* Airbnb → Nights booked
* eBay → Gross merchandise volume

Purpose:

* Align decisions across company

---

## **7. Magic Moment**

* Key moment when user “gets value”

Examples:

* Facebook → see friends
* Airbnb → find great place
* eBay → find desired item

Goal:
→ Get users to this moment **as fast as possible**

---

## **8. Focus on Marginal User**

* Don’t optimize for power users
* Optimize for users about to churn

Growth drivers:

* **Resurrected users + churn reduction**
* Not just new users

---

## **9. Virality**

### Model 1:

Virality =

* **Payload** (people reached)
* **Conversion rate**
* **Frequency**

Examples:

* Hotmail → high frequency + conversion
* PayPal → high conversion

---

### Model 2 (Funnel):

Steps:

1. Import contacts
2. Send invites
3. Click
4. Signup
5. Repeat

→ Defines **K-factor**

Important:

* K > 1 → viral
* K < 1 → not viral

But:
→ **Retention > virality**

---

## **10. SEO Basics**

1. Keyword research (demand, competition, value)
2. Links (authority)
3. Internal linking

Key insight:

* Small changes → huge impact

---

## **11. Email, SMS, Push**

### Rule 1: Deliverability

* Avoid spam
* Maintain reputation

---

### Rule 2: Relevance

* Don’t send generic messages
* Use **triggered notifications**

Example:

* First “like” notification → high engagement

---

### Rule 3: Avoid Noise

* Newsletters ineffective
* Personalization matters

---

## **12. Growth Tactics Principles**

* Remove friction
* Optimize conversion flow
* Test constantly

---

## **13. Internationalization**

* Remove growth barriers early
* Build scalable systems
* Target future markets, not current only

---

## **14. Execution Speed**

* Run many experiments
* Iterate fast

Quote idea:
→ “Good plan executed today > perfect plan later”

---

## **Final Takeaways**

* **Retention > everything**
* Find **magic moment**
* Define **north star metric**
* Optimize for **marginal users**
* Growth = **experiments + speed + persistence**
