# **Lecture 10: Culture (Brian Chesky, Alfred Lin)** 

---

## **Core Idea**

**Culture = foundation for scaling company**

---

## **1. What is Company Culture**

Culture =

* **Core values + behaviors**
* How people act daily
* In pursuit of **mission**

---

## **2. Why Culture Matters**

* Guides decisions
* Aligns team
* Builds trust
* Defines what to do / not do
* Helps hire & retain right people

Insight:
→ Strong culture → better performance

---

## **3. Culture Drives Outcomes**

* Beliefs → actions → habits → results
* Culture = **input**, results = output

---

## **4. How to Define Culture**

Start with founder:

* Personal values
* People you like working with
* Mission alignment

Rules:

* Must support **mission**
* Must be **unique**
* Must be **real (not generic words)**

---

## **5. Core Values**

* Not generic (e.g., “integrity”)
* Should be **specific + actionable**

Example:

* Zappos → “Deliver wow through service”

---

## **6. High-Performance Teams**

Key elements:

* Trust
* Healthy conflict
* Commitment
* Accountability
* Results

---

## **7. Culture as Daily Habit**

* Not slogans
* Must be practiced daily
* Embedded in hiring, reviews, decisions

---

## **8. Hiring for Culture**

* Hire for **values + skill**
* Reject even strong candidates if misaligned

Rule:
→ Skills can be trained, values cannot

---

## **9. Founder Role**

* Culture starts with founders
* Founders = **DNA of company**

Insight:
→ Early team shapes long-term culture

---

## **10. Airbnb Lessons**

### **10.1 Founding Team**

* Choose **exceptional co-founders**
* Team should push each other

---

### **10.2 Culture is Designed**

* Not accidental
* Must be intentional early

---

### **10.3 Mission First**

Example:

* Airbnb → **“Belong anywhere”**

Rule:
→ Hire people who **believe in mission**

---

## **11. Culture vs Short-Term**

* Culture slows short-term speed
* But ensures long-term success

Insight:
→ Culture = long-term investment

---

## **12. Key Values Example (Airbnb)**

### 1. **Champion the mission**

* Work for purpose, not job

### 2. **Be scrappy / creative**

* Constraints → innovation

---

## **13. Culture in Decisions**

* Reject bad culture fit (even if strategic gain)

Example:

* Didn’t acquire competitor due to culture mismatch

---

## **14. Culture & Brand**

* Culture (internal) → Brand (external)

Rule:
→ Employees define brand

---

## **15. Communication of Culture**

* Repeated storytelling
* Reinforce mission constantly

CEO role:

* **Define vision + repeat it**

---

## **16. Early Culture Practices**

* Write values early
* Hire slowly
* Maintain high bar

---

## **17. Strong vs Weak Culture**

* Not “good vs bad”
* **Strong vs weak alignment**

---

## **18. Key Insight**

* Great companies excel in:

  * Product
  * Technology
  * Brand
  * Culture

---

## **Final Takeaways**

* Culture = **how company behaves daily**
* Define early, reinforce constantly
* Hire for **mission + values**
* Strong culture → **long-term advantage**
* Founders = **culture creators**
